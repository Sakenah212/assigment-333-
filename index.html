About the Teacher

Hello! I’m Ms. Sakinah, and I have been a passionate kindergarten teacher for over 10 years. My goal is to create a nurturing and joyful environment where children can explore, learn, and grow. I believe that every child is unique, and I strive to foster creativity, curiosity, and a love for learning in each of my students. Whether we are painting, singing, or discovering new stories, I always aim to make learning fun and engaging. I look forward to working with both you and your child throughout this exciting school year. Please feel free to reach out to me anytime at ms.sakinah@school.com if you have any questions or concerns.
